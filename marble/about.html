About Us

Marmario has a wide range of products and we export the marbles we provide from various manufacturers in Turkey to many countries of the world in the form of blocks, slabs and cut-to-size products.

Marmario means, "flash, sparkle, gleam, of any darting, quivering light." (Liddell & Scott, 1871) and it is thought "marble" could be derived from Ancient Greek name "marmario".
